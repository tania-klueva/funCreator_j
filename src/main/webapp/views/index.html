About

The Fun Creator game studio invites fans of the best rest. Want to spend your time active? We have a bunch of different games for every taste! Are you tired after a business day or just want to relax? What could be better than watching a favorite movie with popcorn in a cozy atmosphere! You will get a separate room, so you do not have to worry about extra noise. We offer 6 rooms: 4 rooms for watching movies and video games, a billiard room and a room for table tennis. All rooms have comfortable sofas and a desk. In the room, you can additionally order any board games and food or drinks.
